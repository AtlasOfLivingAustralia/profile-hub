Peer Reviewing Profiles

  1. Review process: The Administrator assigns Reviewer roles to those who will provide peer review of proposed major changes. Reviewers can view the draft changes made by Editor(s), and provide comments in the review comments section at the bottom of the profile. Reviewers cannot otherwise modify the profile. Two levels of review are suggested:
    1. scientific review of the content and taxonomy; and
    2. editorial review of the grammar, syntax etc
  2. Comments: Reviewers should provide relevant feedback in the ‘comments’ section at the bottom of the View Profile page. These comments will be visible to anyone assigned the role of Editor or Reviewer for a specific collection. Editors will then have the opportunity to respond to reviewer feedback.
